ROCKY SAVES THE UNIVERSE

You are an Eridian engineer, and you have no eyes. Nobody on your world has ever had them, because your world has never had light. You know a room the way you know a song: you make a sound, and you listen to what comes back.

Press E to pulse. The wave leaves you at the speed of sound, so the far wall arrives late. Rock is loud. Grit is nearly silent. And sound crosses stone — badly, but it crosses — so the machines humming behind a wall will show you where the wall ends.

Something is wrong with the heat.
